Step 6: Underwater Visibility Score (UVS)

A custom 0–100 quality index measuring underwater image visibility. The formula combines four weighted components to produce a single actionable score.

UVS = 0.20 × Brightness + 0.30 × ColorBalance + 0.25 × Contrast + 0.25 × Sharpness
Brightness
Weight: 20% — Mean luminance distance from ideal (128)
Color Balance
Weight: 30% — Channel deviation from gray-world equilibrium
Contrast
Weight: 25% — Standard deviation of luminance
Sharpness
Weight: 25% — Laplacian variance (edge energy)
